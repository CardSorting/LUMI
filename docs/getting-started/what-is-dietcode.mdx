DietCode is an open-source AI coding agent that runs in your editor. It gives you direct, transparent access to frontier AI with no limits, no surprises, and no model ecosystem lock-in. See every decision. Choose any model. Control your costs.

## What DietCode Can Do

DietCode operates directly in your codebase with your permission:

- **Read and write files** - Navigate your codebase, create new files, and make targeted edits. You approve every change before it happens.
- **Run terminal commands** - Execute shell commands, run tests, install packages, and debug errors in real-time.
- **Use a browser** - Launch a browser to test web apps, capture screenshots, and interact with pages.
- **Connect external tools** - Extend capabilities with [MCP servers](/mcp/mcp-overview) for databases, APIs, and documentation.
- **Run from the command line** - Use the [DietCode CLI](/dietcode-cli/getting-started) for headless automation, CI/CD pipelines, and shell workflows.

## Workspace Integration

DietCode isn't a separate app; it's a part of your local development environment. It has a direct, 1-to-1 parity with your project structure:

- **Local File Access**: DietCode reads and writes files directly in your workspace. It sees what you see—your `.env` files, your `node_modules`, and your local Git history.
- **Local Tool Execution**: When you approve a command, DietCode runs it in your local terminal. It has access to your `npm`, `python`, `go`, and any other tools you have installed.
- **Local Persistence**: All project context, task history, and AI "memory" are stored on your machine in a local **SQLite database** (`broccolidb.db`). Nothing about your codebase is stored on our servers.
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: DietCode acts as an extension of your own actions. It proposes a change, you review it in your editor's diff view, and only when you click **Approve** does it modify your files.
