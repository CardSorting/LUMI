# The DietCode Edge

In a world of AI coding assistants, DietCode stands out by being a true **agentic partner** rather than just an autocomplete tool. This guide explores the "wedge" that DietCode offers over traditional solutions like GitHub Copilot, Cursor, and other AI editors.

## 🚀 True Agentic Autonomy

Most AI assistants are reactive—they wait for you to type or ask a question. DietCode is **proactive**:

- **Observe-Plan-Act**: DietCode doesn't just suggest code; it analyzes the problem, proposes a multi-step plan, and executes it (with your approval).
- **Architectural Maturity (Deep Planning)**: For complex tasks, use `/deep-planning`. DietCode performs a silent investigation, builds a detailed implementation plan, and tracks progress via a visual **Focus Chain** todo list.
- **Parallel Productivity (Worktrees)**: Unlike other AI editors that take over your screen, DietCode can work in a separate **Git Worktree**. This lets the agent build a feature in one window while you continue coding uninterrupted in your main window.
- **Multi-Tool Integration**: DietCode can run shell commands, launch browsers, read documentation, and even create sub-agents to solve complex sub-problems.

## 📐 Architectural Sovereignty (The Deep Wedge)

DietCode is the only agent that acts as a **Forensic Architect**, enforcing your project's standards at the engine level:

- **Universal Guard**: A real-time policy layer that understands your project's **Layering Rules** (Domain, Core, Infrastructure). It prevents side effects in sensitive layers during planning and enforces strict architectural purity.
- **Autonomous Self-Healing**: Every edit is followed by a **Refactor Healer** pass that aligns imports, synchronizes tags, and ensures structural consistency.
- **Stability Telemetry**: DietCode calculates a stability score for every change, reporting "Net Health" impact and architectural violations directly in your activity stream.
- **Forensic Impact Analysis (Blast Radius)**: Before cutting code, DietCode calculates the "Blast Radius" of a change by analyzing historical correlations and recursive dependencies in its **Forensic Knowledge Graph**.
- **Long-Term Directive Memory (Landmarking)**: Unlike other agents that lose context in long sessions, DietCode uses **Landmarking** to compact old history into high-density "Cognitive Snapshots," preserving your core project goals forever.

## 🛡️ Privacy & Zero-Trust by Default

While other tools require you to upload your codebase to their servers for indexing, DietCode is **local-first**:

- **No Server-Side Code Storage**: Your source code never touches DietCode's servers. The AI reasoning happens on your machine via your chosen API provider.
- **Local Persistence**: Task history and codebase context are stored in a local SQLite database (**BroccoliDB**) on your physical drive.
- **Project Wisdom (Memory Bank)**: DietCode maintains a persistent **Memory Bank** of your project's unique patterns, lessons learned, and architectural context, all stored locally.

## 🔌 Extreme Extensibility (MCP & Hooks)

DietCode is built on a foundation of open standards, giving you total control over its "superpowers":

- **Custom Interceptors (Hooks)**: Run your own scripts at every stage of the agent's lifecycle (`PreToolUse`, `UserPromptSubmit`, etc.). Enforce enterprise compliance, inject dynamic context, or create automated CI/CD gates.
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**: Connect DietCode to any tool in your stack—databases, API docs, or project management tools—using a growing ecosystem of community servers.
- **Model Agnostic Freedom**: Switch between Claude, GPT, Gemini, or local models (Llama 3) without provider lock-in.
